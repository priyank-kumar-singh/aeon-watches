Self Charging Battery

We all know that various smartwatches are available in the market with numerous functionalities, but their battery life is mediocre. In our product, the battery is solar powered backed by a motion powered charging system. 1.5 hour of exposure to sunlight will be enough to fully charge the battery. After the battery gets fully charged it lasts for 4 days. And there’s no question of users not going out in sunlight for 4 days. We also provide a motion charging system wherein the battery will be charged by the kinetic movements. Thus it's hardly possible that the battery will get discharged. Nonetheless considering the worst case scenario we have provided traditional charging for backup.

Health Diagnostic System

As we all know that health is of utmost importance and keeping in mind the recent scenario of pandemic heath tracking has become very essential. .Individuals with COVID-19 experienced lower heart rate variability, little variation in time between heart beats, in contrast to COVID-negative individuals, the study found. The SPO2 level and pulse can be used to alert the user about the possibility of covid-19 infection. SPO2 reading can be used to diagnose an anemic person. Heart rate readings can be used to infer heart conditions. Thus it can be used to diagnose or atleast alert the user of symptoms to various ailments.

Covid-19 Alert

After detecting SPO2 and heart rate monitoring, the covid detecting system will consider this data to detect covid and alert the user of possibility of covid-19 infection. A continuous beep will be heard for 2 seconds and the voice assistant in watch will say”Covid Alert!” twice and then “Covid Alert!!! Please consult a doctor.” will be displayed on the screen.

FAQs

My Aeon Watch is not connecting to the app ?

Keep the Aeon progear app updated to the latest version
Close all the programs on the watch > Stop and restart bluetooth > Connect again.

My Aeon Watch is receiving no alerts, texts, or phone calls?

Make sure smart reminders on the app are on and synced to the Watch Storm.

My Bluetooth keeps getting disconnected?

Make sure there is not more than 7m distance between bluetooth on the phone and the watch.
There is no obstacle between the watch and the phone.

Which functions of the Watch Storm need bluetooth to work?

Activity tracker, Sleep Monitor, Alarm Clock, Sports Mode, Call & Text
